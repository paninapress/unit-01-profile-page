Hello, I’m Nina.

Background

An introvert with vibrantly colored hair who loves sales, networking, and tech, crazy right?! I mean, an introvert that’s into tech makes sense but sales and networking involve *gasp!* dealing with people! Yes, I know, I’m a bit of a walking contradiction.

When I’m not at the keyboard, I enjoy living a nomadic lifestyle visiting and growing my chosen family wherever I can with my husband and our adventure cat, Boe. We bring our fur baby out on various new experiences, such as hiking new trails in remote areas near the Canadian border or kayaking and sailing around. I’m also a bit of a foodie as well as a rucking and festival-going enthusiast.

As an INTJ I’m 75% introverted but you might not know that from meeting me. My secret as a social introvert is finding that finely tuned balance of being social with a purpose. And my purpose is to connect. I find joy in connecting people to other people and people to products.
