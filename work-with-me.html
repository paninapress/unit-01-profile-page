How I work

I'm a very methodical person so we'll take things step by step.

Step 1: Discovery Session

First, we'll figure out exactly what you need.

Step 2: Sitemap

Next I'll draft out our blueprint for how we'll organize your website's information.

Step 3: Prototype

At this step, I'll set up an interactive prototype so you can get a feel for the functionality much better than if I was just to try to explain it.

Step 4: Design

After the prototype is approved, it's time to style everything up with font, colors, and images.

Step 5: Development

Here I'll retreat to my coding cave and bring your beautiful site to life like a mad scientist!

Step 6: Delivery

Finally with everything tested and approved, we'll move everything over to your hosting account so you can start sharing your brand new site with the world!
